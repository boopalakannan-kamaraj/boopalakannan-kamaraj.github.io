Cladding Sheet Estimator

✦Built with Cursor
Role · UX Lead · Freelance
Year · 2025
Cemintel · Façade estimator
Read case study ↗
The brief

Estimators were doing maths on paper, in Excel, on the back of brochures. Cemintel needed a tool that let a project manager spec a façade — panels, trims, fasteners, joins — in the time it takes to drink a coffee.

Designed the input model around how trades actually measure: by elevation, not by SKU. Paired with Cursor AI to ship a working estimator in weeks, not quarters — the design and the build kept pace with each other.

The unlock was treating the estimate as a living document: change a panel size and watch fasteners, off-cuts, and lead-time slide in sympathy.

Proshort Notetaker

✦Built with Cursor
Role · Sr. UX Designer
Year · 2025
Lightweight recorder · Desktop app
Read case study ↗
The exploration

A desktop recorder that quietly notices whatever call is running on your laptop — Zoom, Meet, Teams, a Slack huddle, even a WhatsApp call — and offers to capture it. No bot to admit, no platform lock, no CRM weight around it.

After the call it hands back a summary, owned action items, a searchable transcript and auto-highlights, with a meeting-details rail kept in plain sight. Ask anything across every call you've recorded.

The whole thing was prompted into a working, clickable state in Cursor — researched and built, not mocked. The prototype was the spec: the “lightweight vs. heavy” argument made by being lightweight.
